Cryo-EM Image Reconstruction

Reconstructed an image of a macromolecule from 500 noisy projection images obtained through cryo-electron microscopy (cryo-EM). The dataset contained various noise distortions, point-spread function (PSF) effects, and misalignments in orientation and translation. Processed the data to achieve the best possible structural representation by applying preprocessing techniques, alignment methods, and reconstruction algorithms.
